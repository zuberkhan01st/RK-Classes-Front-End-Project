About RK CLASSES

RK CLASSES is dedicated to providing top-notch education for students in 5th to 8th grades. Our mission is to offer a comprehensive and engaging learning experience across all subjects, including Science, Mathematics, and Language Arts. With a focus on building a strong academic foundation, we aim to prepare students for future academic challenges and foster a love for learning.

Led by the experienced and passionate educator, Mohit Lakhani, RK CLASSES offers personalized attention to ensure each student's success. Our curriculum is designed to be both thorough and stimulating, helping students excel in their studies while developing essential skills.

Join us at RK CLASSES and be part of a supportive learning community committed to your child's educational growth and success!
